# FixMzansi Executive Summary

## What we are building
FixMzansi is a trust-first marketplace for home services in South Africa. The first wedge is Cape Town solar services.

## Why this is the right first market
- solar demand is strong in Cape Town
- trust is a major buying factor
- customers want fast access to verified providers
- the category is valuable enough to support strong economics
- the product can expand into adjacent trades later

## Strategic thesis
Win one city, one category, one trust loop, then expand.

## First wedge
- City: Cape Town
- Category: solar installation and solar-related home energy services

## Business outcome sought
Build a profitable marketplace with:
- repeat demand
- verified supply
- strong local SEO
- WhatsApp-native distribution
- future payments and financing expansion
