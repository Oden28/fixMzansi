# FixMzansi Cape Town Product Spec

## Product name
FixMzansi

## Launch city
Cape Town

## Initial trade category
Solar installation and solar-related home energy services

## Product vision
FixMzansi Cape Town helps homeowners and small businesses discover, verify, compare, book, and pay trusted solar professionals.

## Primary user problem
People need solar installation and maintenance, but they struggle to:
- find reputable installers
- compare pricing transparently
- verify quality and credentials
- avoid low-quality or unresponsive providers
- get fast booking and reliable communication

## Target users
### Demand side
- homeowners in Cape Town and surrounding suburbs
- small businesses needing solar installs or maintenance
- landlords and property managers
- households looking for backup power or reduced grid dependence

### Supply side
- solar installers
- electricians who also handle solar work
- battery and inverter specialists
- small solar contracting businesses

## Core jobs to be done
1. Find a trusted solar installer quickly
2. Compare a few vetted providers
3. Understand likely price ranges
4. Book an assessment or installation
5. Confirm the provider is legitimate
6. Pay securely
7. Leave a verified review after completion

## Core value proposition
"Trusted solar installers in Cape Town, instantly discoverable and bookable."

## Product experience direction
The product should feel premium, calm, and trustworthy — not loud or cluttered. The UI should signal reliability, competence, and clarity.

### Color system
- Primary: deep ocean blue (`#0F4C81`)
- Accent: solar teal (`#14B8A6`)
- Action: warm amber (`#F59E0B`)
- Background: midnight slate (`#020617`)
- Surface: deep slate (`#0F172A`)
- Success: emerald (`#10B981`)
- Error: rose (`#F43F5E`)

### UI character
- mobile-first
- spacious cards
- clear trust badges
- high-contrast text
- simple action hierarchy
- polished but not flashy

## MVP scope for Cape Town launch
### Must have
- landing page focused on Cape Town solar
- request form for solar installation / maintenance
- pro profile pages
- verification badge system
- matching and ranking
- booking flow
- admin approval dashboard
- review system
- contact / notification flow
- basic payments or deposit capture
- request detail page with live matches
- booking button on match cards
- notification queue on booking
- admin moderation UI
- customer and pro dashboard surfaces
- public trust/how-it-works page
- real auth login/register flows
- role-based routing to the correct dashboard
- live KPI cards and market health metrics
- review submission after completion
- booking status transitions
- visible notification inbox
- graceful error and loading states

### Should have
- suburb-based landing pages
- photo uploads for roofs / sites / installs
- service-area filters
- basic pricing guidance
- WhatsApp notifications

### Not in MVP
- BNPL
- full finance products
- mobile app
- insurance and warranties
- deep automation
- multi-city launch

## Success criteria
The Cape Town product is working if:
- users can find good installers faster than by asking around
- pros are willing to join and complete jobs
- bookings can be tracked end-to-end
- trust signals reduce hesitation
- the first category can produce repeat demand and referrals

## Operating principles
- trust first
- mobile first
- data-light
- suburb aware
- simple flows
- manual operations before over-automation
- optimize for conversion and repeat usage
