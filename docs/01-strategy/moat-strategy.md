# Moat Strategy

FixMzansi's moat is not just "a marketplace". It is a trust system built around Cape Town solar work.

Primary moat layers:
- Verification: approved pros are surfaced above unverified or rejected providers
- Local density: narrow Cape Town focus improves matching quality and response times
- Workflow lock-in: request, booking, notifications, and reviews create a repeated operating loop
- Reputation data: completed jobs and reviews improve future matching and trust
- Operations layer: admin moderation and queue handling keep supply quality high

Defensibility comes from the compounding value of better local data, better provider trust, and better fulfillment visibility.
