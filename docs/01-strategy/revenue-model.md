# Revenue Model

FixMzansi revenue should be aligned with trust and fulfillment, not just lead resale.

Recommended revenue streams:
- Commission on completed bookings
- Featured placement / boosted visibility for verified pros
- Subscription tiers for pros who want more leads, analytics, or profile enhancements
- Premium customer add-ons later, such as priority matching or emergency callouts

MVP recommendation:
- Start with a simple per-booking take rate or referral fee
- Keep the customer experience friction-light
- Make paid placement depend on verification and performance so the marketplace stays trustworthy
